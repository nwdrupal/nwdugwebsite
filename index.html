North West Drupal User Group

About us

We are a group for Drupal users in the North West of England

What we do

The aims of North West Drupal User Group are:

  • To bring together Drupal users and promote the use of Drupal in the North West of England
  • To facilitate the sharing of knowledge about Drupal and other related and supporting technologies

Members

We have no formal membership list, but all are welcome, irrespective of geographical locaiton.
